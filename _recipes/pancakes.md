---
title: Pancakes
---
## Ingrecients
* 1.5 cup Flour
* 3.5 tsp Baking Powder
* 1 tsp Salt
* 3 tbsp Butter, melted
* 1 tbsp White Sugar
* 1 and 1/4 cup Milk
* 1 Egg

## Directions
1. In large bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar
2. Make a well in the center
3. Pour in mild, egg, and melted butter
4. Mix until smooth
5. Griddle at 275 degrees

Serve and Enjoy!
