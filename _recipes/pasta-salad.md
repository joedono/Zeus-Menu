---
title: Pasta Salad
---
## Ingredients
* Elbow Macaroni
* Tuna
* Miracle Whip
* Canned Corn
* 2 Eggs
* Italian Salad Dressing

## Directions
1. Boil the macaroni to cook it. Cool with cold water and set aside
2. Boil eggs, then chop them into little pieces
3. Mix miracle whip with tuna
4. Mix all ingredients in serving dish

Serve and Enjoy!
